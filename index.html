Cakes We Offer

We offer a variety of appetising flavours to create a sensory fiesta in your mouth! These range from Pineapple to Chocolate Devil cake, classic Victoria sponges to Walnut and Coffee!

Pineapple

A light pineapple sponge which can be served as a naked cake, or with either fresh cream, buttercream or fondant covering. Multi-layered and filled with delectable fillings, this will add a splash of joy to any get-together or fulfil your guiltiest cravings!

Victoria Sponge

A delightful cake served with elegant frosting, perfect served with tea or other beverages. It has a light regular flavour with a sponge perfect for a quick bite or a graceful gathering. The classic filling of jam and cream or buttercream are regularly served.

Chocolate Devil

A rich chocolate cake which is popular with the younger crowds! Packed with flavour and gorgeous to look at, it can be served with a chocolate glaze, or else chocolate buttercream, vanilla buttercream, fresh cream also make top picks! Personalised messages and designs can be organised.

Coconut and Pineapple

Grated coconut with pineapple chunks, a perfect combination of sweet and sticky to satisfy the pickiest of palates. The flavoured sponge can be served with buttercream, fresh cream or frosting coats, with personalising for all occasions.

Vanilla

A timeless classic baked with vanilla bean provides the perfect base for any occasion. It can be served with fresh fruit in the form of a naked cake, or else smothered with buttercream, fresh cream or frosting coats, with personalisation for all occasions.

Biscoff

A unique caramlised flavour with subtle hints of spice to tantalise your tastebuds. It can be served with fresh fruit in the form of a naked cake, or else smothered with buttercream, fresh cream or frosting coats, with personalisation for all occasions.

Carrot

Grated carrots with juiced flavours added to the sponge results in a wholesome cake for all the family. The flavoured sponge can be served with buttercream, fresh cream or frosting coats, with personalising for all occasions.

Walnut and Date

Pieces of date and walnut ground and added to the sponge to create a rich taste. This can be served with buttercream, fresh cream or frosting coats, with personalision for all occasions.

Oreo

Gound oreos added to the cake batter results in a unique chocolate cake with decoration to match. This can be served with buttercream, fresh cream or frosting coats, with personalision for all occasions.
